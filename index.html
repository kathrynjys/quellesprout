Hello and Introduction

Welcome to my brand new, clean, fresh, yet to be properly decorated and decoupaged, art page!
I have been wanting to start an art page for years now, and honestly just never got around to it. I didn’t think I’d do a grand job with one anyway, so the motivation was extremely low. But lately I’ve just really been feeling ready to share, beyond ready to have a voice, and ready to properly document my experiences and feelings about them. And what better way to embrace those feelings than to finally start the art page I've always wanted!

About Me

My name is Raquelle Ivory Lovelace, and I am a 22 year old from Orlando, Florida, currently residing in Mount Pleasant, Michigan. I am the mom to a bold little 15 (almost 16) month old, Luna June Lovelace, and future wife to my favorite dude, who also happens to be the father of my small human, Dorian Basner. I am an illustrator, stay-at-home mom, and a big enthusiast of plants, women, art history, cute things, nerd things, and social justice things.

I am excited to meet you, to be starting this journey of art and words, and beyond excited to share my life and experiences with you! I hope you enjoy the stories and lessons learned that are to come here, and feel free to also enjoy my life via my Instagrams.
